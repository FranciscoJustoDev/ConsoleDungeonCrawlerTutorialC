Auto dungeon crawler

player          2 or 3 enemies

inventory (somehow... good lord)

3 types of room
    fighting
    treasure
    resting

basic items

floors make up the dungeon

1- player starts in the 1st floor
2- one of 3 type of room is chosen at random
3- resolve the room action
4- move the player down one level
    increase enemy attack and health
    increase item quality or item itself
    change room type spawn percentage



when player dies game asks if you want to continue.
    if yes then reset things :)
